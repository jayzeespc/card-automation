Jump to the 2025 Panini Select Football checklist and team set lists

2025 Panini Select Football marks the latest installment of the popular multi-tiered set that always delivers a delightful run of parallels, a roster of robust insert sets, and plenty of signatures.

Collectors should expect to find two autographs and one memorabilia card in every hobby box, as well as 15 Prizm parallels, six inserts, and two Field Level base cards, the scarcest of the five base tiers.

2025 Panini Select Football Checklist Overview

Select is always among the largest base sets across all major sports card releases and again clocks in at 500 cards separated into five 100-card tiers, which each have different designs.

Each tier gets increasingly more rare (e.g., cards #1-100 are easier to find than cards #301-400), and each tier also has its own set of parallels, with some parallels exclusive to certain tiers.

The parallel lineup includes a variety of colors and patterns and will again include the popular Zebra Prizms on base cards and some inserts, including Select Score Throwbacks.

Collectors will find autographs on Select Signatures and Rookie Signatures, both of which will have many parallels.

Jumbo Rookie Signature Swatches combine signatures and jersey material.

On average, an insert card is seeded into every other pack, so collectors will naturally have a lot of options to choose from.

Neon Icons put players under some bright lights.

Color Wheel cards return, bringing collectors a warped background of rainbow colors.

Panini opts for old-school creativity in Starcade, putting images of players into the screen of an arcade cabinet.

The creative juices continue to flow in Alter Ego, which reimagine players in a different form, such as speedy wide receiver Tyreek Hill running as fast as a cheetah.
